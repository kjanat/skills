## Ionospheric Plasma Parameters

| Parameter               | Formula                                          | Value                                               |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Plasma frequency        | omega_pe^2 = n_e*e^2/(m_e*eps_0)                 | ~10 MHz (ionosphere); ~1 kHz (upper magnetosphere)  |
| Electron cyclotron freq | omega_be = \|e\|B_0z/m_e                         | ~2pi x 1 MHz at equator (B~30 uT); ~2x toward poles |

## Q-bursts

Exceed Schumann background by factor >=10. Rate: 50-100/hr.
Correlated with large +CG flashes that produce sprites.
